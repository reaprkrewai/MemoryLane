window controls are present in every render state. Currently `<TitleBar />` is only
mounted inside `<AppShell>` (AppShell.tsx:7), and `<AppShell>` only wraps the Unlocked

missing.

just the happy path. The regression was introduced by commit 40f6ea6 (Phase 05-01 PIN
security), which restructured App.tsx from "single AppShell wrapping everything" to
"six sibling state branches", without carrying the title bar into the non-unlocked
branches.

Output: `<TitleBar />` lifted above the state switch in App.tsx; AppShell simplified to
render only the sidebar + content layout (no longer mounts TitleBar); PinSetupScreen and
PinEntryScreen layouts adjusted so their centered cards are not clipped by the 48px title
bar above them.

          </div>

        {/* State 4: PIN not set, show setup */}

          </div>
